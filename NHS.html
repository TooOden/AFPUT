1 when you use the nhs website, we use various technologies to collect information automatically – such as your ip address.

7 they store information about how you use the website, such as the pages you visit.
8 email subscriptions if you sign up for one of our email subscription services, we will hold the information you submitted (such as your email address) for as long as we are providing you services.

10 you will be asked for your consent when you submit your data to us.

15 we will hold the information for as long as we are providing you services.
